About

Hi there!

Welcome to my sandbox.

Through this blog, I hope to share my thoughts on analytics and quantitative investment. The latter is something I’m keeping track of - with skin in the game. It aligns with my core belief that any analysis should be accompanied with corresponding bets with the appropiate risk-rewards ratios - fan of Nassim Taleb in this regard.

See here for my investment framework and performance.

1 of the motivations in starting this blog is for me to think, learn, experiment, code, write and most importantly place well-informed bets/investments through the lens of my models + analysis.

Over the course of my life, I hope to stay insatiably curious. As a curious & keen learner, I’m constantly striving to improve myself through structured and unstructured learning.

Please contact me at jironghuang88@gmail.com if we share common interests!
